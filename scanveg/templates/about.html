About Dataset, CSV, and YTAPI

Features a curated CSV file with detailed recipes, and YouTube API for videos for visual cooking guides. The model is trained with an images dataset containing 15 different vegetables. This rich resource enhances our ML model's ability to accurately identify vegetables and suggest diverse, engaging recipes.

Content

Key Features:

        Image Recognition: Using cutting-edge ML models, the app accurately detects the type of vegetable in your image.

        Recipe Suggestions: Receive tailored recipe recommendations based on the identified vegetable, with detailed instructions provided in text format.

        Video Tutorials: Access curated YouTube videos for visual step-by-step guides on preparing your chosen recipes.

        CSV Integration: Recipes are sourced from a comprehensive CSV file, ensuring a diverse table of culinary options.
